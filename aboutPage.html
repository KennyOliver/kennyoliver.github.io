UI Design Guide

This is my first ever website which works 'well' on phones and tablets (small & large) .
However, the experience is optimised for desktop Chrome & Safari .
Why? Chrome is the most used browser in the world, and both of them are 'WebKit' browsers.
This means supporting one will immediately support the other!

Opera & Edge (Chromium) should work too. FireFox will work, but only on iOS/iPadOS devices — the Android version doesn't use WebKit. IE not so much.
